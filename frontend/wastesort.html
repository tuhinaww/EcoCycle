Waste Sorting Guide

Blue Bin - Plastic

  • • Plastic bottles and containers
  • • Plastic bags and wrappers
  • • Plastic cups and utensils
  • • Shampoo and detergent bottles

✓ Clean and empty before recycling

Green Bin - Paper

  • • Newspapers and magazines
  • • Paper bags and cartons
  • • Office paper and envelopes
  • • Books and notebooks

✓ Keep dry and free from food contamination

Yellow Bin - Glass

  • • Glass bottles and jars
  • • Glass food containers
  • • Glass drink bottles
  • • Glass decorative items

✓ Rinse containers, labels can remain

Gray Bin - Metal

  • • Aluminum cans and foil
  • • Steel and tin cans
  • • Metal bottle caps
  • • Small metal appliances

✓ Empty and rinse food containers
