EcoSort Game

Test your recycling knowledge! Drop the items into the correct bins to earn eco-points and learn about proper waste sorting.

How to Play

  • 1. Identify the waste item that appears on screen
  • 2. Drag it to the correct bin (plastic, glass, paper, or cardboard)
  • 3. Earn points for correct answers and learn why items belong in specific bins
  • 4. Race against the clock to improve your eco-awareness score!

Benefits

  • Learn proper recycling practices in a fun, interactive way
  • Understand the environmental impact of proper waste sorting
  • Compete with friends and family to improve recycling habits
  • Earn eco-points that can be redeemed for rewards in the EcoCycle app
